We offers classes to students aged four years old and up through the Glencoe Park District. All students enroll as beginners and as they attain physical and mental growth, knowledge and experience they will progress through different belt levels.
Students may also choose to join the Pride Dojo competition team through which they will compete at AAU (Amateur Athletic Union) sanctioned tournaments. Competition begins at the state level with the opportunity to qualify for regional, national and international competition. The competition team is independent from the Glencoe Park District.

Tournament Schedule/Applications

Pride Dojo is a club member of the AAU (Amateur Athletic Union) and its students may compete within AAU sanctioned karate competitions. All competitors must join AAU with a nominal membership fee. The competition year begins with a "registration tournament" in the fall and continues with a State tournament in the winter and Regional tournament in the spring, which are qualifying competitions for the National tournament which takes place in June. The State and Regional tournament are held at the College of Lake County in Grayslake and the National tournament is in a different city every year. Pride Dojo also competes yearly at the AAU Junior Olympics in late July/August. Pride Dojo students have achieved great success at the National and International level, including numerous individual and team medals. More information can be obtained at the AAU Karate website and Senseis will provide all registration material for competitions or they can be downloaded below.
